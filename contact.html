How can I apply for a PhD position?

PhD applications are processed through the university's graduate admissions system. Please visit the graduate school website for application requirements and deadlines.

Do you offer internship opportunities?

Yes, we offer summer internship programs for undergraduate and graduate students. Applications typically open in January for the following summer.

Can I visit the lab?

We welcome visitors! Please contact us in advance to schedule a lab tour and meet with our researchers.

How can I collaborate with your lab?

We're always open to collaboration opportunities. Please send us a detailed proposal outlining your research interests and potential collaboration areas.
