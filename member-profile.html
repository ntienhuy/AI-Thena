    Biography

    Dr. Sarah Chen is the founding director of AI-THENA Lab. Her research focuses on neural architecture search and efficient deep learning. She received her PhD from MIT and has published over 100 papers in top-tier conferences.

    Prior to founding the lab, she worked as a Research Scientist at Google DeepMind where she contributed to several breakthrough projects in automated machine learning. Her work has been recognized with numerous awards including the NSF CAREER Award and the MIT TR35.
